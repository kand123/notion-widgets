Your New Home Journey - Final Steps                  
        
            
                             
            

🎉 Congratulations!

            

Your dream home is almost ready. Here's everything you need to know for the final steps.

        
                 
            
                

🏠 Homeowner Orientation

                
                    
                        Scheduled: No less than 2 business days before your closing date                     
                    

Our Customer Service Team will contact you to schedule your orientation. This isn't a "blue tape walk" – our Construction Management Team has already handled all necessary touch-ups!

                    

What to expect: A comprehensive walkthrough covering operation and maintenance of your new home.

                    

Who can attend: Contract signers and your Buyer's Agent are welcome.

                
            
            
                

⚡ Transfer Utilities

                
                    

Transfer utilities to your name effective on closing day to avoid service interruptions.

                    
                        Need utility provider information? Contact me for community-specific details.                     
                
            
            
                

💰 Final Purchase Price

                
                    

Work with your EDGEhomes Agent and lender to verify your final purchase price before closing.

                
            
            
                

📋 Review Seller Disclosure Documents

                
                    

Important documents are available in your Dropbox folder. Please review all materials as updates may have been added since contract acceptance.

                    
                        
                            Action Required: Review all disclosures, waivers, and property information before closing.                                                      
                    
                
            
            
                

📅 Closing Date Extensions

                
                    
                        Important: Late fees apply if you cannot close by your scheduled date. Extensions require signed addendum and are approved at Seller's discretion.                     
                    

Contact me immediately if you need an extension to avoid contract default.

Receive keys immediately after signing closing documents

📮 Mailbox Keys

                
                    

After closing, contact your nearest Post Office for mailbox key information.
